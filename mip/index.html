The indupro Hat

📥 my indupro vCard

The hat I have been wearing the longest… juice news, rootstocks and ingrediens are seedlings that sprout from under that hat.

indupro

The company started by my father back in 1976… a brokerage firm with a focus on fruit juices, concentrates, oils & essences and other by-products. My core business is connecting you (either producer or purchaser) to the right partner: Not just for the product, but also with the right mentality and professional ethics.

juice news

“daily free curated headlines for the juice industry”… a free newsletter I have been sending out to an ever growing audience (currently 1500+) of professionals in the juice and beverage industry. If you do not receive it yet, please subscribe.

rootstocks

With tag-line “The Origins of Wine” rootstocks shares the stories of various renowned winemakers. I have been interviewing them since October of 2019 and this paid-for mailing is being shared on a monthly basis.

ingrediens

Throughout my years in the ingredients industry, I have encountered many nuisances and irritations. Rather than letting them be I am building ingrediens to tackle the ones that cost most time, ask for extra manual labor that can be automated. It is a work in progress, but with great ambitions.





The The Fork Hat

📥 my The Fork vCard

I am a Business Developer for The Fork since October 2018. With a focus on Food Integrity on Blockchain, The Fork is helping companies and organizations to understand and implement Blockchain in their food supply chains.

Besides that we bring together all stakeholders in the Food Supply Chain during a very hands-on summit called Strike Two Summit.

Want to know more? https://thefork.online and https://striketwosummit.com
